DSGNS "Nothing Is Waiting For Us"

"Nothing Is Waiting For Us" was released in 2024. It was the first DSGNS album written collaboratively by the full lineup, with Daniel Maldonado contributing all lyrics and vocal arrangements. The record expanded on the foundation built by Ceremony, pushing further into atmosphere, precision, and dynamic structure.

The album explores themes of isolation, repetition, and personal ruin through deliberate rhythm and restrained aggression. It reflects the band’s evolution from calculated heaviness to something more deliberate and expressive.

"Nothing Is Waiting For Us" captures DSGNS in full control of their sound and vision, balancing intensity with clarity and purpose.

Track listing:

  1. Bloom (Music Video)
  2. Dark Cloud
  3. Ghost Runner
  4. Second Death

Released in 2024

DSGNS "Ceremony"

"Ceremony" was recorded and released in 2020. It was the first DSGNS release to feature Daniel Maldonado on vocals. The music had already been written prior to his joining the band, with founding guitarist Stephen (formerly of Thumbscrew) leading its composition. Maldonado contributed all lyrics and vocal performances.

The album captures the band’s precision-driven sound, blending elements of math metal and hardcore with a focused, rhythmic intensity. Ceremony served as a transitional release for the group, laying the groundwork for the fully collaborative writing approach that would define Nothing Is Waiting For Us.

  1. Crooked Teeth
  2. Dead Husk
  3. Zealot (Music Video)
  4. Ceremony
  5. Hungry Tombs
  6. Bolter

Released in 2020

Thumbscrew "Within Hearts of Redemption" EP

"Within Hearts of Redemption" was recorded and released in 2006. It marked a shift in Thumbscrew’s sound toward a more melodic and atmospheric direction while maintaining the precision and aggression established on All Is Quiet.

The album introduced more layered arrangements and reflective lyrical themes focused on redemption, faith, and personal struggle. It represented the band’s final and most complete statement before their dissolution, bridging technical intensity with emotional weight.

Thumbscrew "All Is Quiet"

"All Is Quiet" was recorded and released in 2004. It was the debut full-length from Thumbscrew, a mathcore and technical hardcore band active during the early 2000s Myspace era. The album captured the raw and dissonant energy that defined that period, with fractured rhythms, shifting time signatures, and an unpolished urgency.

The release showcased guitarist Stephen’s early songwriting approach that would later evolve in his next project, DSGNS. All Is Quiet remains a snapshot of the era’s underground chaos and a reflection of the band’s formative sound.
