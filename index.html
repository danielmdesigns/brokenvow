Angelhead Digital Cover Art

Angelhead formed in 2001 in Norfolk, VA. Matt Frazier (vocals), Keith Hernandez (guitar and vocals), and George Nicholls (drums) wrote some heavy songs and made an EP with Bob Gurske at the infamous Winter Sound recording studio. They played a handful of shows and had several different friends help on bass and 2nd guitar, including Dave Mesa, Ronnie Dodson, and Kevin Zoldan.

The EP was originally released on CD in 2001, and recently re-released on streaming platforms in 2024.

Track listing:

  1. Parasitic
  2. Frontline
